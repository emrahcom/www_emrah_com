		libwine-ldap

		libwine-sane

*** winetricks

	- Betiğin son halini indirmek için
		cd .wine
		mkdir winetricks
		cd winetricks
		wget http://www.kegel.com/wine/winetricks
		chmod u+x winetricks

	- cabextract
		su -
		aptitude install cabextract
